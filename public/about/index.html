About Me

Who am I?

Hi! I’m Davis Vaughan, a Master’s student studying Mathematical Finance at the University of North Carolina at Charlotte. I enjoy all things R. I’m also a coffee enthusiast, as most R users seem to be.

What else?

I helped co-author two R packages with Matt Dancho:

  • tidyquant - Bringing financial analysis to the tidyverse
  • timetk - A collection of tools for working with time series

I’m also interested in Data Science and Quantitative Finance. I’ll be looking for job opportunities post graduation in May 2018.
